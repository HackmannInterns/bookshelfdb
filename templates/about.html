About

Background

This library is a free and open source project for anyone to store and catalogue their books digitally. This project came about by a book enthusiast named John Hackmann. John has been a collector of books for many years, and has accumulated thousands of books that have been published over the century. As the library grew, the need for a catalogue and searching website was in need. he gathered up developers to help him build a digital library where he can freely know where each book is stored and can easily bring in new books to catalogue for his entire library.

Technologies

Flask, Gunicorn, Docker, Datatables, Python.

We use Open Library to access to gather data on the books users enter onto our database. It has been a helpful tool and website in gathering data on whatever book that you so find in the Hackmann Library.
